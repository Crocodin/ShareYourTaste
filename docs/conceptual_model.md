# Conceptual Model — SYT (Share Your Taste)
### Overview
The conceptual model describes the main entities of the SYT application, their attributes, and the relationships between them. SYT is a music rating and sharing platform where users can rate albums and songs, comment on content and profiles, view charts, and receive notifications.
